Partnership Terms

What this partnership includes

Everything below is part of the proposed annual integration between Majesty International Schools and Mokhtareon Academy.

Investment & payment

  • Tuition may be paid in four installments across the academic year, aligned with the school’s semester calendar.

Student support & enrichment

  • Dedicated WhatsApp groups with round-the-clock support for enrolled students.
  • Complimentary getXplain (AI for kids) subscriptions for all participating students.
  • Summer internships at the Mokhtareon office for the top five performing students.
  • Guidance for Grades 10–12 students pursuing a Bachelor’s degree abroad, through our sister company.

School assets & ownership

  • Mokhtareon will establish an electronics laboratory that remains the property of the school.
  • The school retains ownership of Mokhtareon instructional materials used on site.
  • Science and physics teachers receive tools and resources to teach alongside the curriculum.

Events & competitions

  • Two school-wide science fairs per year, covering all participating grades.
  • Full management of the end-of-program graduation ceremony for student projects.
  • Support for any science-related competitions the school enters.
  • Competition participation under the school’s name in robotics, ECPC, and ICPC.

Operations, trips & collaboration

  • Weekly progress reports from instructors to school leadership.
  • Educational trips inside and outside Cairo as part of the annual plan.
  • Ability to run integrated sessions that connect technology with other school subjects.
  • Mokhtareon can act as a marketing partner to maximize the value and visibility of daily in-school sessions.
